Twin Lakes CO 82 Resurfacing Project - Please be advised that there is a road resurfacing project planned for this summer, starting on April 21st, that will impact traffic from the intersection of Hwy 24 and CO 82 through approximately one mile east of the village. Expect delays of up to 20 minutes, speed limit reductions, and lane closures as needed. For more information, visit https://www.codot.gov/projects/co82twinlakesresurfacing - There is contact information at that site if you have questions.
